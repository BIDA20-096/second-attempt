Hotel de Paris Monte-Carlo

There are those exceptional luxury hotels which one day stop being an address and become a legend. The Hôtel de Paris is one of these magical places.

Hotel Hermitage Monte-Carlo

The Hotel Hermitage Monte-Carlo is one of the most intimate luxury hotels in the Principality. It offers a discreet and elegant atmosphere in a beautifully preserved “Belle Epoque” decor.

Hotel Metropole Monte-Carlo

Ideally located in the “Golden Square,” a short walk from the Casino, the Hotel Metropole Monte-Carlo enjoys a central and favoured position between the sea and the mountains. •

Monte-Carlo Beach

A legend of the 1930s revisited by India Mahdavi. The Monte-Carlo Beach offers a resolutely modern experience of life, combining the spirit of the Roaring Twenties on the Riviera with the privacy of a luxury holiday.
